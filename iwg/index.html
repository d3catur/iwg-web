Information Warfare Group

The Information Warfare Group is for Midshipmen that are interested in learning about Securing Computer Networks, Cryptography, and Hacking. The IWG competes in several Cyber competitions throughout the year including capture the flag events and the National Cyber Defense Exercise hosted by the NSA.
